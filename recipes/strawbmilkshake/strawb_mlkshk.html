Strawberry Milkshake Recipe

Description

This classic and simple recipe consists of just a few ingredients but will leave you feeling satisfied and content with a smile on your face. **Can also be adapted for gluten intolerance**

Ingredients

  • 8 whole strawberry
  • Cream (whipped)
  • 150g of vanilla ice cream
  • 200ml of milk (substitute for nut milk if needed)

Directions

  1. Put the vanilla ice cream, milk and 7 strawberries into a blender and blend until smooth
  2. Pour the combined mixture into a tall glass
  3. Next, layer your whipped cream on top of the milkshake in the glass
  4. Garnish with the 1 remaining strawberry
  5. Enjoy!
